About Me

Hi, I'm Santiago Velasco, a 22-year-old Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Colorado Springs (UCCS), originally from Venezuela.

I created Velasco Media with a simple goal: to help businesses establish a strong online presence through modern, responsive, and user-focused websites. In today's digital world, having a professional website is one of the most effective ways to increase a business's visibility, reach new customers, and build credibility. My mission is to make that possible for businesses of all sizes.

Beyond client work, this website also serves as my personal portfolio, where I showcase projects that demonstrate my skills in web development, programming, and software engineering. Whether you're a business owner looking to grow your online presence or a recruiter interested in my technical abilities, you'll find examples of my work and the passion I bring to every project.

I'm always learning, building, and improving, and I'm excited to collaborate on projects that create meaningful impact.
